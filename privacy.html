Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

AI Staffing Solutions ("we", "us", "our") provides AI receptionist and communication services to businesses in Ireland. Our website is aistaffingsolutions.net.

Data We Collect

When you book a call or submit an enquiry, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and business details. This information is used solely to arrange and conduct your onboarding call.

When our AI receptionist handles calls on behalf of our clients, it may collect caller details including name and phone number for the purpose of booking appointments and handling enquiries.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to: respond to your enquiry, schedule onboarding calls, send confirmation emails and SMS reminders, and improve our service. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely within the European Economic Area (EEA). Booking records are retained for 12 months and then deleted. Call logs are retained for 30 days unless a longer period is required by law.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. You may also object to processing or request data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at seana@aistaffingsolutions.net.

Cookies

Our website uses no tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party advertising scripts.
